About Me:
My name is Sam, a web developer, creator, problem solver and musician. Originally a skier hailing from the Northeast, I now find myself coding, biking, paddleboarding, and playing music in Chicago.

I enjoy utilizing JavaScript to build functional, data-driven, cool looking websites and web applications. I have a crazy appetite for learning new things and stretching the all important brain muscles. I am very interested in building upon my development skills with new frameworks, deployment techniques, testing suites, and different languages.

Music was my first passion and, outside of developing, I can often be found sipping a snobby craft beer twiddling knobs on a synthesizer.
